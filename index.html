How LiftShare works

1. Find or offer

Search for rides going your way or offer your empty seats to others.

2. Connect

Message the driver/rider to confirm details and arrange pickup.

3. Ride together

Meet up, verify with OTP, and enjoy the journey together.

4. Rate & repeat

Leave a review and build your community reputation.

Your safety is our priority

Verified profiles

All users must verify their phone number and email.

OTP verification

Unique one-time password ensures you meet the right person.

Real-time tracking

Share your ride details with trusted contacts.

In-app chat

Communicate securely without sharing personal numbers.
